About Me

I've been an animal lover since I was a little girl growing up in Switzerland. We always had cats at home, and I knew early on that animals would always be part of my life.

After moving to San Francisco, I built a career in creative fields and added pet sitting and dog walking to my work. It has brought me so much joy.

I have two cats of my own, Olive and Rosie, so I know exactly how it feels to hand your fur baby over to someone else. I bring the same care, attention, and love to every pet I look after, whether it's an overnight stay, a drop-in visit, or a walk through the neighborhood.

I offer pet sitting at your home, drop-in visits, private walks, and neighborhood group walks. Photo updates are always available upon request.

Please read: My rates reflect the cost of living in San Francisco, allowing me to continue providing reliable, professional care for your pets in the city. Weekend and holiday surcharges apply.

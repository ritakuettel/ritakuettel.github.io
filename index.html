About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm Rita, a professional pet sitter and dog walker living and working in San Francisco. I grew up in Switzerland with a love for animals, and now I make my living caring for pets here in the city.

I have two cats of my own, Olive and Rosie, so I know exactly how it feels to trust someone else with your furry family. Follow their adventures on Instagram: @oliveandrosiesf.

I offer pet sitting at your house, drop-ins, private walks, and neighbourhood group walks. I can send photo updates during my services upon request. I'll treat your pet like it would be my own.

Please read: My rates reflect the cost of living in San Francisco, allowing me to continue providing reliable, professional care for your pets in the city. Weekend and holiday surcharges apply.
